How can You Enter Colgis BD?

First, Let's Register with BtoJ HRD.

Steps

Member Registration

To be a registered member of this site. By doing so, you may receive job offers from Japanese companies.

Offers from Japanese Companies

You may get job offer from Japanese companies based on the information you have provided during the registration process.

Employment Request

You can view the details of the job offers and can also request for some changes in employment conditions.

Adjust the Interview Schedule

You can request to change date and time of the given interview schedule.

Interview

Interview with Japanese team members.

Notification of Recruitment or Non-recruitment

Confirmation after the interview process is completed.

Agreement

If accepted, then an agreement will take place with detail conditions and explanations.
